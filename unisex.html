Unisex
Erotic fashion geek show UNISEX is group of three artists who perform in night clubs
Task
Design a website where it could be possible to meet the group, watch videos of their recent performances and of course place an order for a performance
Concept
My idea was to show the full range of the artists' abilities, that's why I decided to focus on their photos and chose the color scheme that matches the night-club atmosphere (dark background with bright colorful flashes)
Logo
There was a request to make a logo design. The client’s vision was that i should be just a text with an interesting font. Surprisingly, the first and only version was accepted
The result
The end product is a modern, intriguing and interesting website
